# BLUF (Bottom Line Up Front) Principle

> Optimize information retrieval for instant comprehension and quick scannability.

- **Title/Header**: Put the category or core keyword first.
  - Default: `Core Keyword: Short Title`
- **Sentence**: Start with bold key details followed by the explanation.
  - Default: `**Key Detail**: explanation`
  - With Category: `**[Category] Key Detail**: explanation`
  - With Field: `Field: **Key Detail** - explanation`
- **Paragraph**: Put the conclusion in the first sentence.
- **Document**: Place the TL;DR, Overview, or Summary at the top before detail sections.

---

*Note: Formatting (symbols, delimiters, emojis) serves as examples or defaults. Conventions, standards, and guidelines take precedence over defaults.*
