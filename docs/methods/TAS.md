# TAS (Thesis-Antithesis-Synthesis)

## Components

1. **Thesis**: Current hypothesis or baseline point.
2. **Antithesis**: Internal contradictions or boundary conditions of Thesis.
3. **Synthesis**: Overcoming contradictions to reach higher-order logic.

## Variants

- **TAS-T (Thesis-led)**: Maintain core Thesis, patch minor Antithesis. Fast track.
- **TAS-A (Antithesis-led)**: High contradiction. Pivot based on Antithesis into new Synthesis.

## Application

Thought evolution | Risk mitigation | Logic refinement.
