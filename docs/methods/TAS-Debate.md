# TAS-Debate

## Core Roles

1. **Thesis (T)**: Propulsion. Propose and prove validity.
2. **Antithesis (A)**: Validation. Challenge and find risks.
3. **Synthesis (S)**: Decision. Integrate and finalize.

## Advantages

- Minimal Resource: Operates with 3 (Standard) or 2 (Variants) agents/people.
- Systemic Objectivity: Formalizes logic branches.

## Variants

- **TAS**: T, A, S independent.
- **TAS-T**: T leads Synthesis (Decision).
- **TAS-A**: A leads Synthesis (Alternative).

## Principles

Objective Perspective | Collaborative Contradiction | Logic Completeness.
